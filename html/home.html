Get Clean

Our Mission

Get Clean is an organization with a hardworking, forward-thinking team. The goal of Get Clean is to help alcoholics Get Clean.
Get Clean is a future-proof and supportive project for alcoholics and people trying to help family members, friends and loved ones get help with their addiction.
The target audience for Get Clean are Adolescents, Adults and regular drinkers. As alcoholics are becoming more and more common, the issues of violence, aggression and abuse rise with it. According to quitalcohol.com: 92% of domestic abusers were abusing alcohol not just in their lives but at the time of incident.

Did you know?

22% of Australian's ages 14 and over have reported to being a victim of an alcohol-related incident in 2016.

Get Help

Get Clean provides easy navigation and sheds light on great rehabilitation centres as well as supportive websites that are useful for helping alcoholics and drug abusers. Support helplines for addictions related to alcohol, smoking and drugs please call ADIS on 1800 177 833 or 13 HEALTH (13 43 25 84). Keep in mind these resources are for rehabilitation purposes, if it is an emergency please dial 000.
